When did Biryani first appear in the Indian subcontinent?

Biryani is believed to have been introduced to the Indian subcontinent by Persian or Central Asian travelers and conquerors. While its exact origin is debated,biryani likely appeared in the Indian subcontinent between the 13th and 16th centuries, evolving into a celebrated dish during the Mughal era. (Source: Wikipedia)
